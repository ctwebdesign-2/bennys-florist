About

At Benny’s Florist we have hand-crafted bouquets and floral arrangements made by our expert florists using the finest flowers and foliage sourced from Irish growers and on the international markets.

We cater for all family occasions: engagements, weddings, anniversaries, birthdays and other family occasions at a range of prices to accommodate your budget.

Benny’s Florist also offers wonderful seasonal options for St Valentine’s Day, Easter, Hallowe’en and Christmas or any other occasion you wish to celebrate.

For the business world, Benny’s Florist is pleased to provide flowers for hotel lobbies, corporate reception areas and special events … we ae happy to design a competitive package to meet your companies needs and to exceeds your expectations.

Follow Benny’s Florist on Facebook and Instagram to keep up-to-date on our latest creations and special offers.
