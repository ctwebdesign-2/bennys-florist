About

Here at Benny’s Florist, you can find many different types of beautiful hand-crafted bouquets made by the best experts in the Tullamore area.

We provide packages for birthdays, anniversaries, and funerals.

We also currently provide different seasonal packages for Valentine’s Day, Christmas and Easter. We provide these offers at different prices to suit your budget, and if you need any help with picking out something we are available by phone and social media.

We also provide a delivery service to anyone in Tullamore and surrounding areas.
